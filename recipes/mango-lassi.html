Mango Lassi

Mango lassi is a delicious creamy drink with mango, yogurt, milk, a little sugar, and a sprinkling of cardamom. Itâ€™s cool and refreshing on a hot day!

  1. 1 cup chopped very ripe mango, frozen chopped mango, or canned mango pulp
  2. 1 cup plain yogurt
  3. 1/2 cup milk
  4. 4 teaspoons honey or sugar, more or less to taste

That's it! A pretty simple, blender drink.
